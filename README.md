# Hi, I'm Pascal Kimes 👋

## 👨‍💻 Introduction

I'm a Master's student in **Analytics & Artificial Intelligence (MAAI)** at ESMT Berlin, with a strong interest in **data science, econometrics, and machine learning**.

My work focuses on applying **statistical methods, machine learning models, and data analysis** to solve real-world problems in areas such as consumer behavior, finance, and strategic decision-making.

I enjoy building **end-to-end analytical pipelines**, from data cleaning and exploratory analysis to predictive modeling and interpretation.

Currently, I am particularly interested in:

- Machine Learning & Predictive Modeling
- Causal Inference
- Data-Driven Strategy
- AI applications in business and economics

## 📂 Featured Projects

### 📊 Consumer Preference Modeling – Music Festival Analysis
Choice-based conjoint analysis to understand consumer preferences for music festival attributes.  
Methods include **multinomial logit modeling and willingness-to-pay estimation**.

### ✈️ Airline Reviews Sentiment Analysis
Natural Language Processing pipeline analyzing airline reviews to extract insights about customer satisfaction.

### 🚆 EU-Rail Passenger Satisfaction
Factor analysis and clustering applied to passenger satisfaction survey data to identify key drivers of satisfaction.

### 🤖 Causal Inference & Machine Learning
Replication and extension of academic research using **causal forests and econometric methods**.
